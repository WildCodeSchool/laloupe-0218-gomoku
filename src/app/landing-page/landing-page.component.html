Gomoku, also called 5 in a row, is an abstract strategy game.

Traditionally, it is played by putting black and white stones on a Go board using 15x15 or 19x19 intersections. Players take turns and place a stone of their color on an empty intersection. The goal is to form an unbroken chain of 5 stones (and no more) vertically, horizontally or diagonally.
